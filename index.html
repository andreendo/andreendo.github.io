Andre Takeshi Endo

Short bio: Received a bachelor's degree in Analysis of Systems from the Universidade Federal de Mato Grosso do Sul (FACOM-UFMS), Brazil in 2005. I also hold M.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science from the University of Sao Paulo (ICMC-USP), Brazil, awarded in 2008 and 2013, respectively. My advisor was Prof. Adenilso Simão. During my PhD studies, I was a visiting scholar at the Paderborn University, Germany and Pontifical Catholic University of Rio Grande do Sul (PUCRS), Brazil. In 2019, I worked as a postdoc at the Aarhus University, Denmark, under Prof. Anders Møller. From 2013 to now, I am an Assistant Professor at the Federal University of Technology - Paraná (UTFPR), Cornelio Procopio, Brazil. Since I left my hometown Alvares Machado in 2002, I have pursued my professional passions for computers, programming, and research.
Research interests:
  • Automated Software Engineering with emphasis on software testing and dynamic program analysis
  • Mobile Apps, Node.js/JavaScript applications, and Web Services
